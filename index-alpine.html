At AcademIQ Excellence, we understand that students learn in many ways - books, videos, and in-person classes and that each method has it's limitations.  Books may either not provide the appropriate level of detail or they may overwhelm with too much information, videos are generally focused on one specific problem or topic and in-person classes often don't provide the individualized attention required to master a concept.  AcademIQ Excellence specializes in personalized, online tutoring in select science, technology, engineering and math subjects at both the high school and college levels and helps to fill the gaps in mainstream learning.

Our Formula For Success

Foundational Skills

We emphasize important foundational skills in mathematics that, once mastered, provide the building blocks for more advanced topics.

Patterns/Processes

Whenever possible, we highlight patterns and processes, that can be applied to provide consistency in formulating solutions to problems.

Critical Thinking

We encourage critical thinking to evaluate problems, create a methodology for solving them, and finally, assess the correctness of the final answer.
